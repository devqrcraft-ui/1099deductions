OnlyFans Tax 1099 Tax FAQ

Does OnlyFans withhold taxes?
No. OnlyFans sends a 1099-NEC for earnings over $600 but does not withhold any federal, state, or self-employment taxes. You are responsible for quarterly estimated payments.
Is the OnlyFans 20% fee deductible?
Yes. The 20% platform commission is a direct cost of earning income — deductible as "commissions and fees" on Schedule C Line 10.
Can OnlyFans creators deduct costumes?
Yes, if the costumes are used exclusively for content creation and would not be worn in everyday life. Costumes clearly for content work (uniforms, character costumes) are deductible business supplies.
Do OnlyFans creators pay self-employment tax?
Yes. OnlyFans creators pay 15.3% SE tax on net profit (after all deductions). You also deduct 50% of SE tax paid from your gross income, which partially offsets the burden.
What is the quarterly tax deadline for OnlyFans creators?
2026 quarterly deadlines: April 15 (Q1), June 16 (Q2), September 15 (Q3), January 15 2027 (Q4). Use IRS Form 1040-ES.
