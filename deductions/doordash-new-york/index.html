2026 Tax Summary — DoorDash — New York

DoorDash drivers in New York pay 15.3% SE tax + federal + NY state tax. On $40,000 net: approximately $5,652 SE tax + $3,200 federal + ~$2,200 NY = ~$11,052 total. Quarterly payment: ~$2,763.

New York DoorDash drivers face some of the highest tax burdens in the country — state income tax up to 10.9%, plus NYC city tax up to 3.876% for residents. This checklist covers every federal and New York-specific deduction you can claim on IRS Schedule C in 2026.

New York-Specific Deductions

NYC Congestion Pricing Fee Fees paid to enter the NYC Congestion Relief Zone (below 60th Street in Manhattan) during active DoorDash deliveries are 100% deductible as a business expense. Keep payment receipts from E-ZPass or the MTA.
NYC Bridge & Tunnel Tolls All bridge and tunnel tolls paid during active deliveries — MTA bridges, Holland Tunnel, Lincoln Tunnel — are 100% deductible even when using the standard mileage rate.
New York State Quarterly Filing Unlike Texas or Florida, New York requires separate quarterly estimated tax payments to the NYS Department of Taxation and Finance in addition to IRS payments. Use Form IT-2105. Q1 deadline: April 15.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do New York DoorDash drivers pay city income tax?
Yes. NYC residents pay city income tax of 3.08%–3.876% on net profit in addition to New York State income tax of 4%–10.9%. All Schedule C deductions reduce both.
Are NYC congestion pricing fees deductible?
Yes. Fees paid to enter the Congestion Relief Zone during active DoorDash deliveries are 100% deductible as a business expense on Schedule C.
Do New York DoorDash drivers file quarterly state taxes?
Yes. Quarterly estimated payments to both the IRS and the New York State Department of Taxation and Finance are required if expected annual tax liability exceeds $1,000. Use Form IT-2105 for state payments.
What is the New York state income tax rate for gig workers in 2026?
New York state income tax ranges from 4% to 10.9% depending on net income. NYC residents pay an additional 3.08%–3.876% city tax on top of state.
