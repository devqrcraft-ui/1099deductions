Frequently Asked Questions
What can OnlyFans creators deduct on taxes in 2026?
Camera and lighting equipment, costumes and props, home office (up to $1,500/year), phone and internet bill (business %), editing software, hair and makeup for shoots, and platform fees.
Does OnlyFans send a 1099 tax form?
OnlyFans sends a 1099-NEC for US creators earning $600+. You must report all income on Schedule C even if you do not receive a form.
Can OnlyFans creators deduct clothing and costumes?
Yes, if the clothing is specifically purchased for content creation and not suitable for everyday wear. Keep receipts and document the business purpose.
Is a ring light or camera fully deductible for OnlyFans?
Yes. Equipment used to create content is a business expense deductible on Schedule C. Large purchases may be fully deducted in year one under Section 179.
How much tax does an OnlyFans creator pay?
Self-employment tax: 15.3% on net profit. Plus federal income tax on net profit after deductions. Deductions directly reduce net profit and therefore both SE and income tax.
