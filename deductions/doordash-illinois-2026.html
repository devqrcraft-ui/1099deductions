What can Illinois DoorDash drivers deduct in 2026?

Do DoorDash Illinois drivers pay quarterly taxes?

Yes. All miles driven for DoorDash are deductible at $0.725 per mile for 2026. Track every mile from the moment you accept a trip until drop-off.
