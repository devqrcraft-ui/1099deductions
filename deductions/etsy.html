Frequently Asked Questions
What can Etsy sellers deduct on taxes in 2026?
Materials and supplies, Etsy listing and transaction fees, shipping costs, photography equipment, home studio/office, packaging, software subscriptions, and advertising.
Does Etsy send a 1099 form?
Etsy sends a 1099-K if you have 200+ transactions and $20,000+ in sales (federal threshold). Many states have lower thresholds. Report all income regardless.
Can Etsy sellers deduct materials and supplies?
Yes. All materials used to create your products — fabric, clay, wood, paint, thread — are fully deductible as cost of goods sold or business expenses on Schedule C.
Can I deduct a home studio for my Etsy business?
Yes. If you use part of your home exclusively for your Etsy business, you qualify for the home office deduction. Simplified method: $5/sq ft up to 300 sq ft = up to $1,500.
Are Etsy fees tax deductible?
Yes. Etsy listing fees ($0.20/item), transaction fees (6.5%), payment processing fees, and any Etsy Ads spending are all deductible business expenses.
