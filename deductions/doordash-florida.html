Frequently Asked Questions
Do Florida DoorDash drivers pay state income tax?
No. Florida has no state income tax. You only pay federal income tax and SE tax (15.3%). This is a significant advantage over drivers in California or New York.
What can Florida DoorDash drivers deduct?
Mileage ($0.67/mile), hot bags, phone and data, vehicle maintenance proportional to deliveries, parking, tolls, and any required equipment.
Do Florida DoorDash drivers pay quarterly taxes?
Only federal quarterly taxes (no Florida state income tax). Deadlines: April 15, June 16, September 15, January 15, 2027.
What is the biggest write-off for Florida DoorDash drivers?
Standard mileage at $0.67/mile. Florida drivers often log high mileage in hot weather — also track extra car washes and AC-related maintenance.
Can Florida DoorDash drivers deduct a cooler bag for hot weather?
Yes. Insulated and cooler bags used to keep food at proper temperature during Florida deliveries are 100% deductible equipment.
