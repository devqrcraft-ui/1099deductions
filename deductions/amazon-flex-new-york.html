📦 Amazon Flex Tax Deductions in New York 2026

Amazon Flex drivers are independent contractors who use their own vehicles to deliver packages. Every delivery mile and required equipment is deductible. This page covers all federal deductions plus New York-specific tax rules for package delivery workers.

New York State Tax: 4%–10.9%
New York has state income tax rates from 4% to 10.9%. NYC residents also pay city income tax (3.08%–3.876%) on top of state tax.
New York Note for Amazon Flex Workers New York City residents pay both state AND city income tax — the combined burden can reach 14.8% on top of federal taxes.

New York Amazon Flex Tax FAQs

What can New York Amazon Flex drivers deduct in 2026?
Mileage ($0.70/mile), cargo organizer, phone, vehicle maintenance, parking, and NYC tolls during active delivery blocks. NYC congestion pricing fees during deliveries are 100% deductible.
Do NYC Amazon Flex drivers pay city income tax?
Yes. NYC residents pay city income tax (3.08%–3.876%) on net profit plus NY state income tax (4%–10.9%).
Do New York Amazon Flex drivers pay quarterly to NYS?
Yes. Quarterly estimated payments to IRS and NY State Department of Taxation are required if annual tax liability exceeds $1,000.
Are NYC parking fees during Amazon Flex blocks deductible?
Yes. All parking fees paid while actively delivering packages for Amazon Flex are 100% deductible business expenses. Keep all receipts.
